What the papers cover

The Wealthy Tenet documents explain why the traditional housing path no longer works for many renters, why rent rarely builds assets, and how a rent-linked Bitcoin accumulation model could realign incentives across tenants, operators, asset managers, policy makers, and communities.

The documents also outline legal, operational, behavioural, and implementation considerations that must be tested before the model is deployed.

Who should read them

These papers are written for housing operators, developers, property managers, economists, Bitcoin thinkers, policy professionals, lenders, investors, and people interested in systems-level economic design.
